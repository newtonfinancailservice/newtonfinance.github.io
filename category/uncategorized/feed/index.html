Pay Yourself First

What does Pay Yourself First Mean?

“Paying yourself first” means that you should pay your own savings and investment accounts first.

Here are some examples:

  • Paying into your RRSPs
  • Buying Insurance like Life Insurance
  • Save for emergencies
  • Set yourself up with Living Benefits
  • Pay off your debts and don’t take on new ones!  (Exceptions to a mortgage or reasonable business loans).

12 Tips To Prevent Financial Stress Over The Holidays

1. Stick to a budget and don’t over spend

2. Shop for sales and don’t pay full price

3. Do a gift exchange and don’t buy for everyone

4. Do a potluck dinner and don’t have it catered

5. Shop online for sales and don’t pay full price

6. Be creative with stalking stuffers and don’t over spend

7. Look for discounted gift cards

8. Use Reward Points

9. Volunteer your time instead of spending all your money

10. Be minimal with your decorating. Use fresh garland from the local shops.

11. Make a list and check it twice, don’t buy extra gifts

12. Do it yourself and put some thoughts into your gifts
